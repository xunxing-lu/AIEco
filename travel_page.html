ğŸš— Transportation Guide

To Mangshi:

  • By Plane: Mangshi has Dehong Mangshi Airport with direct flights from Kunming.
  • By Bus: Long-distance buses from Kunming to Mangshi take about 7-8 hours.

To Shudong Hot Springs:

  • By Car: Rent a car from Mangshi, costing around 100 yuan.
  • By Self-drive: More convenient, follow navigation to Zhefang Town, Mangbang Village.

Note:

  • Respect local customs: Shudong Hot Springs is a traditional bathing place for the local Dai people. Visitors should respect local customs and avoid making noise.
  • Limited facilities: As a natural hot spring, facilities are limited. It's recommended to bring necessary supplies.
